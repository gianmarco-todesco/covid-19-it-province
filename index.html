COVID-19 Italian provinces comparison

This chart shows the new positive cases every day.

You can add provinces by using the pulldown menu on the bottom and remove them by clicking on the small 'x' near the name. Move the mouse over the graph to see actual numbers.

The vertical scale is logarithmic (an exponential growth looks like a straight line).

Data from the Italian "Protezione civile": https://github.com/pcm-dpc/COVID-19

Source code: https://github.com/gianmarco-todesco/covid-19-it-provinces
